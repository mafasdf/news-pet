SENATE EXTENDS DAIRY COMMISSION REPORT The Senate approved legislation toextend until March 31, 1988 the date for the NationalCommission on Dairy Policy to report on the federal milk pricesupport program and the dairy industry.    The report is to recommend whether the AgricultureDepartment should have continued authority to reduce the milkprice support level.    The one year extension was required due to delays insetting up the commission. Reuter
