INDIA'S 1986/87 CASTOR OIL EXPORTS FALL - TRADERS India's castor oil exports areprovisionally estimated at 30,000 tonnes in fiscal 1986/87,ending March 31, against 54,000 tonnes in 1985/86 due to ashortfall in the domestic castorseed crop, private traderssaid.    Drought in parts of the country is expected to reduce thecastorseed crop to a provisionally estimated 350,000 tonnes in1986/87 from 550,000 tonnes in 1985/86, they told Reuters. REUTER
